Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is the first session really free?▾
Yes — completely. The first session is a free assessment where I work through questions with your child to map out their current level and identify weak spots. There's no obligation to continue and no payment required beforehand.
Yusuf is 13 — should I be concerned?▾
It's a fair question and we'd rather you asked it than wondered. Yusuf achieved the top SATs score in his year group and attends one of the country's most prestigious grammar schools. He sat these exact exams very recently, which means his knowledge is current and his ability to relate to younger students is genuine. All sessions take place online, and parents are welcome to observe at any time.
How do sessions work?▾
All sessions are one-to-one and take place online via video call. You'll need a device with a camera and a stable internet connection. Sessions are one hour long and fully focused on your child's needs.
What age groups do you teach?▾
We work with students in Years 4–8, covering KS2 and KS3. This includes 11+ and SATs preparation as well as general subject support.
What's the difference between the 20-minute call and the first lesson?▾
The 20-minute call is just a quick informal chat — no teaching, no pressure. It's a chance to ask questions and decide if Crestline is right for your child. The first lesson is the free assessment session where we actually start working together.
Do you provide materials?▾
Yes — practice questions and structured lesson plans are provided as part of the tutoring. Paid resources and mock tests are also coming soon via our Resources & Mock Tests page.
Can my child get help in more than one subject?▾
Absolutely — just let us know when you register and we can split sessions across subjects.
What if we need to cancel or reschedule?▾
Life happens — just let me know at least 24 hours in advance and we'll reschedule at no cost.
Are group sessions available?▾
Not yet, but they're coming soon! We're planning small-group sessions at a reduced rate for siblings or friends preparing for the same exam. Email us at yusuf-crestlinetutors@outlook.com to register your interest.
